About Me

My learning journey...

From the moment I produced my first lines of code, I knew I was hooked on development. I have never been so interested and taken with something before and since that moment I have been learning through the use of online courses, books, YouTube videos, study groups, and basically anything I can get my hands on. I have been building projects for friends and for fun along the way and I have been truly enjoying aquiring this new skill. It is something I am proud of and want to continue on to be the best developer I can be.

I am comfortable with:

  • HTML
  • CSS, SCSS, BEM
  • Animations
  • JavaScript
  • VSCode
  • Chrome Developer Tools

I have some experience with:

  • Node.js
  • Bootstrap
  • Webpack
  • Firebase
  • SEO
  • Web accessibility (a11y)

At this point in time I am continuing to learn more about Javascript, studying algorithms and have started learning React, which is turning out to be really interesting. I am also starting to explore more intermediate/advanced CSS, both styling and animations.

My goals are, first, to always continue learning and I would like to see myself in a junior developer position where I can apply the skills I have gained and working with people who are better than me and can help me grow. The perfect workplace for me would be a place that takes interest in my development and encourages me to ask questions and to be better than I was yesterday.

When I am not coding you can find me out for a hike with my husband, riding my bike, or hanging out with a book and a cat.
